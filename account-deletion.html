Account Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their Elective Staffing account and associated personal data using one of the following methods:

In-App

Log into the Elective Staffing mobile app and navigate to Account Settings → Delete Account.

By Request

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing click@electivestaffing.com from the email address associated with your account. Please include “Account Deletion Request” in the subject line.

What data will be deleted

Upon request, we will delete your account and associated personal data. Certain information may be retained if required for legal, regulatory, fraud prevention, or operational purposes.
